Privacy Policy

ICT Quiz App – G.C.E. (O/L) ICT
Effective Date: 12 December 2025

1. Introduction

ICT Quiz App (“the App”) is an educational mobile application developed to help students prepare for the Sri Lankan G.C.E. Ordinary Level (O/L) Information and Communication Technology (ICT) examination. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the App.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information.

  • does not require user accounts or logins
  • does not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, location data, or contacts
  • does not collect biometric data
  • does not track users across apps or websites
  • does not transmit personal data to any server operated by us

3. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

To provide core functionality, the App stores certain information locally on your device only, using Android local storage (such as SharedPreferences).

  • quiz attempt counts
  • app preferences (e.g., shuffle questions, confirmation prompts, keep screen awake)
  • purchase / unlock status for model papers

This data:

  • remains entirely on your device
  • is not shared with us or any third party
  • can be removed by uninstalling the App or clearing the App’s data

4. Payments & In-App Purchases

The App may offer optional in-app purchases (for example, unlocking additional model papers). All payments are processed securely by Google Play Billing.

  • We do not collect, see, or store payment card details or billing information
  • Google Play handles payment data according to its own Privacy Policy

https://policies.google.com/privacy

5. Children’s Privacy

The App is intended for educational use by students. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since the App does not collect personal data at all, it is designed to comply with applicable child privacy and student data protection requirements.

6. Third-Party Services

The App may use Google Play services for:

  • app distribution
  • in-app billing

No analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs are used by the App. Google’s Privacy Policy applies to Google Play services:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

7. Data Security

Because the App does not transmit personal data to external servers:

  • there is no risk of personal data exposure from our systems
  • all app data remains under the user’s control on their device

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the effective date and publishing the revised policy.
